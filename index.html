About Teves

Teves is a cutting-edge software that allows users to control their Tesla vehicles using their Apple Watch. By leveraging Tesla's API, Teves enables users to open doors, control the climate, and open the trunk seamlessly.

Getting Started

To start using Teves, follow these steps:

  1. Download the Teves app from the Apple App Store.
  2. Open the Teves app on your iPhone and follow the instructions to link your Tesla account.
  3. Authorize Teves to access your Tesla's controls through the Tesla API.
  4. Once authorized, open the Teves app on your Apple Watch.
  5. Use the Teves app on your Apple Watch to control your Tesla's doors, climate, and trunk.

Features

  • Open Door: Easily unlock and open your Tesla's doors with a tap.
  • Climate Control: Adjust your Tesla's climate settings directly from your Apple Watch.
  • Open Trunk: Open your Tesla's trunk remotely for quick access.
